# Find outliers using IQR method
q1 = past_year['ride_length'].quantile(.25)
median = past_year['ride_length'].median()
q3 = past_year['ride_length'].quantile(.75)
IQR = q3-q1
upper_fence = q3 + (1.5*IQR)
lower_fence = q1 - (1.5*IQR)
outliers = past_year.loc[(past_year['ride_length']<lower_fence) | (past_year['ride_length']>upper_fence)]

Tableau Dashboard

Act¶

The majority of Cyclistic's customers already have annual memberships, making up more than 60% of all rides for the past 12 months. However, when we analyze number of rides on a daily basis, we can see that the gap between riders who are members vs non-members is much smaller on weekends. This is due to an increase of rides by non-members and a decrease by members.

Another thing to note is that casual riders tend to ride for longer periods of time both overall and on a daily basis.
